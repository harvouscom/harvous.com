Mark a passage or phrase, then leave yourself an annotation: a question, a
cross-reference, the thing you don't want to forget next time you read this. Pin
the important ones. Filter by kind — notes, scripture, references, connected.

Your highlights aren't a graveyard. They're how you find your way back.
