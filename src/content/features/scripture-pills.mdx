Type **John 3:16** anywhere in a note and Harvous turns it into a pill — a tappable
reference that shows the passage text on hover. Switch translations per-pill or
across the whole note. Seven translations included: KJV, NKJV, ESV, NIV, NLT, NET,
BSB.

No clunky verse picker. No copy-paste from another app. You write; we keep up.
